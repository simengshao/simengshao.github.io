Simeng (Simone) Shao


I received my PhD in Statistics at the [Data Sciences and Operations](https://www.marshall.usc.edu/departments/data-sciences-and-operations) department in the Marshall School of Business at USC. I was fortunate to be advised by Dr. [Adel Javanmard](https://faculty.marshall.usc.edu/Adel-Javanmard/) and Dr. [Jacob Bien](https://faculty.marshall.usc.edu/jacob-bien/). Before joining USC, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Statistics at [Renmin University of China](https://www.ruc.edu.cn/en).
My research interests centers around developing machine learning tools for solving real-world problems and exploiting data with different structures, such as high-dimensionality and rare features. My work mainly lies in the following areas: multiple testing, high-dimensional statistics, selective inference and dynamic pricing.


I started working as an applied scientist at Amazon since Aug 2022.
